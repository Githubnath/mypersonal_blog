​10. Trust Her Yourself

​You must reciprocate if you want to win over your wife's or your girlfriend's increased trust. Never ask her who else is going or where she is going if she says she is going out with friends.

You'll come out as tyrannical and suspicious if you do it. Put your trust in your companion, and you will receive it in return.

You aren't in a relationship to eavesdrop on each other's locations or activities; rather, you are in it to exchange love and affection.

When your S.O. is out with pals, we advise you to say "have fun!" or "tell them I said hi!" instead.
